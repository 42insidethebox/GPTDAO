About GPTDao

GPTDao is a revolutionary new cryptocurrency token that represents a stock in a tech company that uses AI to make decisions. With a total supply of 21 billion, GPTDao uses a deflationary model where 1% of every transaction is burned to benefit holders.

Our company's mission is to build cool tech that everyone can use and keep innovating every day. With the help of AI, we are able to make decisions that are in the best interest of all stakeholders and ensure the long-term sustainability of our network.

Learn more about our team, partnerships, and future plans by exploring the rest of our website.

Tokenomics

The GPTDao token is built on the Ethereum blockchain and has a total supply of 21 billion. Each token has a value that is determined by market demand. Transactions are processed on the blockchain, and 1% of every transaction is burned to benefit holders.
